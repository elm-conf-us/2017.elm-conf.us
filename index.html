elm-conf is returning to St. Louis on September 28, 2017 for a day of learning, speaking, and connecting with the Elm language community. We will be once again colocating with Strange Loop, and the conference will run on their preconference day. Join the list below to be the first to know when tickets go on sale. See you soon!

Call For Proposals Opens April 10, 2017

The elm-conf call for proposals (CFP) period opens April 10, 2017. This is when you can submit a talk for inclusion in the conference. Submissions will be evaluated blindly by a small panel. Accepted speakers will get a ticket to the conference, lodging, and travel assistance.

If it’s your first time proposing a talk, don’t worry! We’ll provide a round of feedback for all talks submitted before the last three days of the CFP period.
